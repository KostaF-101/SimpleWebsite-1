My Top Four Values

1. Integrity

Description: Integrity means being honest, trustworthy, and consistent in my actions, even when no one is watching.

Where it comes from: I learned integrity from my family and from teachers who emphasized doing the right thing even when it’s difficult.

Why it matters: Integrity helps me build trust with others and stay true to my goals and identity.

Consequences of not following it: Without integrity, I could damage relationships, lose credibility, and struggle to earn respect in school, work, and life.

2. Responsibility

Description: Responsibility means taking ownership of my actions, completing tasks on time, and being dependable.

Where it comes from: This value developed through school, work experiences, and learning how important it is to follow through on commitments.

Why it matters: Responsibility helps me stay organized, reliable, and prepared for challenges in both academic and professional settings.

Consequences of not following it: Ignoring responsibility could lead to missed opportunities, poor performance, and people losing trust in me.

3. Compassion

Description: Compassion means understanding others’ feelings, being patient, and offering support when people need it.

Where it comes from: My compassion comes from personal experiences, friendships, and seeing how kindness can positively impact others.

Why it matters: Compassion helps me build strong relationships and stay grounded in empathy and understanding.

Consequences of not following it: Without compassion, I could become disconnected from others and struggle to maintain healthy relationships.

4. Growth

Description: Growth represents my desire to keep learning, improving, and becoming a better version of myself.

Where it comes from: This value comes from my academic journey, personal challenges, and wanting to push myself beyond my comfort zone.

Why it matters: Growth motivates me to take on new opportunities, learn from mistakes, and stay adaptable in a changing world.

Consequences of not following it: Without growth, I could become stagnant, miss opportunities, and struggle to reach my full potential.
