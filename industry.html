Industry Overview

The insurance industry plays a critical role in protecting individuals, families, and businesses from financial loss. It provides stability, risk management, and peace of mind by offering coverage for health, property, vehicles, life, and more.

Career of Interest: Insurance Broker

An insurance broker acts as an advisor who helps clients find the best insurance policies for their needs. Unlike agents who represent one company, brokers work independently and compare multiple insurers to get clients the best coverage and price.

Ethical Issue in the Insurance Industry

A common ethical issue is the idea for brokers to encourage policies that pay higher commissions rather than those that truly benefit the client. This conflict of interest can harm trust and lead to clients being underinsured or overcharged.

How My Personal Values Shape My Response

My values such as integrity, and responsibility would allow me to always put the client’s needs first. As well, I believe in honesty and transparency, so I would recommend policies based solely on what protects the client, not just what benefits me financially.

Cybersecurity Issues in the Insurance Industry

Insurance brokers handle very sensitive personal information, including Social Security numbers, financial records, medical history, and identification documents. This makes the industry a major target for cyberattacks like data breaches, phishing, ransomware, and identity theft.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

To protect client data, insurance professionals must follow strong cybersecurity practices such as:

• Using encrypted communication • Using multi-factor authentication • Constantly updating software and security patches • Training employees to recognize phishing attempts • Storing data securely and limiting access • Following Insustry regulations and state privacy laws
